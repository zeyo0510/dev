- Instalation Guide
Go to Google Chrome and open the Settings menu in the top-right, choose More Tools > Extensions. 
With the Extensions panel open you will find a Developer Mode checkbox, probably on the top-right corner, please enable it. 
Now you will see a new button called "Load unpacked" or "Load unpacked extension", please click on it and then choose the Gravit Designer 
folder that you unzipped earlier. Now you will see Gravit Designer in the list of extensions.
