3DView app

v. 0.11.9
Developed and supported by Awesome Team.
Special credits to three.js project authors and community.

3DView is a lightweight, free and easy offline application for viewing, measuring, editing and sharing 3D files. It is great for checking models for 3D printing. Open or download multiple 3D files; measure; convert units; scale, position or orient; export as STL / OBJ or share on Google Drive™.
Supported 3D file formats: STL, OBJ, AMF, 3DS, PLY, Collada, CTM, VTK and ZIP.

3DView works on many operating systems that support Chrome and WebGL including:

    • Windows: Vista, 7, 8 or later
    • Mac OS X 10.8+
    • Chrome OS
    • Linux: Ubuntu 12.04+; Debian 7++; OpenSuSE 12.2++; Fedora Linux 17
3DView requires Google Chrome v28 or later to be installed and WebGL enabled.
