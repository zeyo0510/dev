Guide 1. What 3DView can do for you.

3DView is intended to make any operations with common 3D files easier. It has a wide range of features from getting 3D models to making them real:
  1. Find 3D models with this guide
  2. Download multiple 3D models from the web (requires additional plugin)
  3. Open 3D models from local disk or from your Goggle Drive storage
  4. Combine multiple models together
  5. Change colors
  6. Check model quality with this guide
    • see inverted and intersecting surfaces
    • see triangles
    • highlight holes
    • detect thin walls
    • look inside
    • see slices
    • measure model size, angles and radiuses
  7. Repair models with this guide
    • make model watertight
    • remove unnecessary shells
    • reduce amount of triangles
    • smooth 
  8. Change units: inch>mm, mm>inch
  9. Translate, rotate and scale
  10. Position model on a 3D printer's platform
  11. Convert 3D models from various formats to STL
  12. Save 3D models to local disk or share on Google Drive
  13. Manage your Google Drive storage right from the app
  14. Make your 3D model real by reading my guide on how to print it
You will find more information on how to do that in the Introduction to 3DView user interface and guides.
