Did you know...

Interior structure of 3D model affects quality of 3D printing!

If your 3D model has thin walls or self-intersections - it might look much worse when 3D printed or not 3D printed at all. 3DView offers tools for inspection of your model interior. You can use clipping planes from the "VIEW" sidebar tab to look inside your model or "Structural Analysis" mode to detect thin walls, self-intersection surfaces and estimate supports volume.
